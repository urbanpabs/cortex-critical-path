Delivery Approach

Horizontal vs Vertical Slicing

🚫

Horizontal Slicing

Complete each layer before integration

Coder: All templates complete
Cortex: Building scorecards...
Kratix: Starting promises...
  • No value delivered until all layers complete
  • Integration problems discovered late
  • Teams work in silos
  • Long feedback loops
  • Risk concentrated at the end

Bake Your Layer, But Deliver Together

Each team owns their layer of the cake. Kratix builds the promises. Cortex builds the catalog and scorecards. Coder builds the workspace templates. But we don't serve layers — we serve slices. Every slice cuts through all three products to deliver complete, end-to-end value to our developer community.

"Nobody wants a mouthful of frosting. They want the whole slice."
